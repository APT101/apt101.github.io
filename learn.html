Welcome to the Learn section of APTT. Here you can learn about the most common tricks attackers use in emails, calls, texts, and face to face situations. You’ll see how they pretend to be trusted people, create fake emergencies, and make their messages look real to steal information or access locations they are not meant to. You’ll also learn how both simple and advanced scams work so you can spot the tactics behind them. Finally, these modules show the core defenses needed to protect yourself against the attackers. These include slowing down, verify through trusted channels, and never acting on a request you didn’t confirm yourself.
